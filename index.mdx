# Welcome to Groupbox

Groupbox is your all-in-one collaboration hub for teams that want to **chat, manage projects, and stay organized effortlessly**. Whether you’re working on school projects, small businesses, or professional teams, Groupbox gives you the tools you need to communicate smoothly, assign tasks, and track progress — all in one place.  

We also value **data privacy, transparency, and trust**. Your information is handled with care, and we encourage you to read our [Terms of Service](/legal/terms) and [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy) to fully understand how we protect your data.
