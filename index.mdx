# Welcome to Groupbox

A collaboration hub for teams that want to **chat, manage projects, and stay organized effortlessly**. Whether you’re working on school projects, small businesses, or professional teams, Groupbox gives you the tools you need to communicate smoothly, assign tasks, and track progress, all in one place.

We also value **data privacy, transparency, and trust**. Your information is handled with care, and we encourage you to read our [Terms of Service](https://groupbox.cloud/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://groupbox.cloud/privacypolicy) to fully understand how we protect your data.
